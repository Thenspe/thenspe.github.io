Co-op Placement with Empulse Geophysics

Points North Landing, Saskatchewan

This is some information about the first portion of my co-op placement, which took us up to Points North Landing in the northern portion of Saskatchewan.

Our job was to carry the magnetotellurgic equipment to the pre-determined testing areas, which were set up on surey lines, then dig in and bury them so Dave or Ryan could take readings.
